# **App Name**: Mombasa Legal Experts

## Core Features:

- Firm History: Display a timeline of the firm's history, from its founding in 1985 to the present day.
- Practice Areas: Detail the firm's areas of legal expertise (e.g., corporate law, real estate, etc.).
- Attorney Profiles: Showcase individual attorney profiles with their expertise and experience.
- Contact Information: Provide key contact details (email: info@kanyij-advocates.co.ke, phone, address) for the Mombasa office.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#003366) to convey trust and professionalism, reflecting the firm's legal expertise.
- Background color: Light blue (#E6F0FF), a very desaturated shade of the primary hue, for a clean and professional look.
- Accent color: Gold (#B8860B), an analogous color, used sparingly to highlight important information.
- Font pairing: 'Alegreya' for headlines and 'PT Sans' for body text to provide a balance of elegance and readability.
- Use simple, professional icons to represent practice areas.
- Maintain a clean and organized layout with clear sections for easy navigation.
